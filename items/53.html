53 Be aware of 65k method limit

Android application is limited by 65k method. You may easily get TOP LEVEL EXCEPTION due to exceeding method limit. To avoid this:
  • Use proguard
  • Don't add playservices blindly in gradle as dependency. Play services comes with a very huge method count if you directly download all of them at once. Use only what you needed.
compile 'com.google.android.gms:play-services-base:7.0.0'
